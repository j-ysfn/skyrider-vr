How to Play
Open https://j-ysfn.github.io/skyrider-vr/ on your phone in landscape mode. No installation needed.

Controls
Tilt phone up → climb
Tilt phone down → dive
Tilt phone left → turn left
Tilt phone right → turn right
On desktop: arrow keys or WASD

Setup Options
Music: ON/OFF (background music in menus)
Sound FX: ON/OFF (engine, ring, crash sounds)
Speed: Normal / Fast
Starting Level: 1 or 2
VR Cardboard: OFF/ON

Levels
Level 1: Fly through 8 rings, 100 pts each
Level 2: Fly through 12 rings, 200 pts each

Scoring
Fly through a ring → score points
Hit a building → lose 50 points, screen flashes red
